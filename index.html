THE MANDATE

A house of record for the discipline of statecraft.

The Institute for Strategic Diplomacy exists to produce policy research that survives contact with a negotiating table. We hold no partisan mandate and serve no single capital — our briefs are built to be useful to any delegation seeking rigor over rhetoric.

Every publication passes through desk review and receives the Director-General's Executive Seal before entering the public record. Nothing reaches the library unvetted.
